Girls Who Code
San Jose State University

GWC @ SJSU is dedicated to fostering a supportive network for women in tech. We are coders and engineers - of all genders - who support the female presence in computing. We run events, from tech talks to socials, and provide opportunities for female engineers to connect and succeed.
